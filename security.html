Safety and
Security

Your safety is paramount here at Murtala Mohammed International Airport 2.

Aviation Security (AVSEC) team


Our certified Aviation Security (AVSEC) team is here to help you. They work constantly to ensure the safety of passengers, concessionaires, and other users of the terminal. They are well-equipped with the skills to handle any scenario. They are also willing to guide you through the airport. Feel free to approach any officers in a light blue shirt and navy blue trousers for any inquiries or concerns. Our team is spread out across every part of the airport, including the cargo operations building and the car parks.

Security Standards


The terminal meets all security standards set by the International Civil Aviation Organization ( ICAO) and the International Air Transport Association (IATA). Medical Emergencies


We have a clinic and a hospital for any passenger seeking medical attention. Both of them operate a 24-hour service to cater to the health needs of all users of the terminal. The clinic can be found near the far entrance of the terminal, in front of the elevator. The hospital can be found outside of the terminal building near the terminal gate. A 24-hour ambulance service to transport those in need of medical services.
